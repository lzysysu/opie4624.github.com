I was sitting at a Willy Porter concert a week ago. During the concert a thought occurred to me, that I hope to explore.
Douglas Adams has a fantastically written article on the decreasing need for religion. He talks of the four ages of sand.
  1. sand -> glass -> lenses -> telescopes
  2. sand -> glass -> lenses -> microscopes
  3. sand -> silicon -> microchips
  4. sand -> glass -> fiber optics -> communications
