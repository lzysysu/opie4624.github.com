Today Adam invited me along on his car purchase adventure for moral support and lifting assistance. The adventure started with a stop a Big Pete's House of Munch where Seth Rogen rented us a U-Haul trailer.  Then we met up with Crazy Charlie, who told us the ins and outs of the parking lot paint striping business in the process of telling us about the car.  After driving back through what appeared to be a town made up entirely of West Bloomington residents, we decided that we had to leave town immediately. A 2 hour drive ended what Adam and I hope is the last trip to Mattoon.
